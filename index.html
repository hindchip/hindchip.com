ABOUT HINDCHIP

Technology Infrastructure For The AI Era

HINDCHIP is the technology brand of Hindustan Chip Technologies Private Limited. We believe advanced computing technologies will define the next generation of economic growth, scientific discovery, industrial competitiveness and national capability.

Our focus is on building long-term foundations in artificial intelligence infrastructure, advanced computing systems and future semiconductor technologies.

We envision a future where India is not only a consumer of advanced technologies but also a creator of world-class computing platforms and intelligent systems.

OUR MISSION

To Help Build The Technological Foundations Required For India's Intelligent Future

HindChip exists to strengthen India's long-term capabilities in advanced computing, intelligent systems and future semiconductor technologies. Our mission is to contribute to the foundations that will power future generations of innovation, industry and scientific progress.
